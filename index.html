About Me

My name is Francine Kyla Mateo, and i was born in pamarawan, malolos bulacan. Growing up in that province my childhood was filled with exciting memories of hangging out with family and friends, always traveling and exploring the beautiful corners of bulacan. Whether it was a quick trip to local spots or a longer journey to new places, I’ve always loved discovering new places. These experience ignited me in continous adventures and that is what am I today.

However, life wasn’t always easy for me . One of the biggest challenges I faced was constantly moving schools because everytime I already feel comfortoble to that place and whenever i already have new friends we always need to move house or school. I never had the chance of staying in one place for so long, and it meant I had to adjust quickly to new classmates, teachers, and environments. At times It felt overwhelming, but this constant change thought me adaptability, and the ability to embrace new Beginnings. It wasn’t easy, but It shaped me into someone who can handle change with grace

Today, at 21 years old, I’m pursuing my Bachelor of Science in Criminology in College. My decision to enter this field wasn’t made lightly, but I’m deeply committed to it. The road to college has been full of challenges, but I know that becoming a law enforcer, wheter in the Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP) or the Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP), is my calling. My cousin, who’s always been a role model to me, Inspired me to take this path.I want to serve my country and make a difference in people’s lives, and this dream keeps me motivate everyday. When it comes to relationships, I’m perfectly happy being single. I’ve come to realize that being in a relationship involves a lot of responsibility, and right now, I want to focus on my studies and personal growth. It’s a time to explore who am I, what I want, and where I’m headed in life without worrying about other obligations. The peace that comes with being single allows me to channel all my energy into achieving my goals.
